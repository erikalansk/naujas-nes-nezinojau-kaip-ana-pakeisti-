Becoming successful on social media

"Reach for top" Photograph by Erika Lanskaronskyte

We think everyone, including you, have ever thought about becoming a social media star. All the opportunities, fame and of course money attract us a lot. However, it’s not that easy to become successful on social media. Only a couple of people are lucky enough to do it extremely fast. But today you will be the lucky one and we will give you some tips and tricks that might help you become a successful person on social media.

Growing your social media audience

It is not a secret, that to have success on SoMe platforms you must have an always engaging audience. It takes a lot of effort to establish such, but it pays off. So, what you have to do is:

  1. Have a plan – know what you want social media do for you, how you are going to accomplish your goals and how you are going to keep up with everything.
  2. Share your story – SoMe users love to follow people who are open about their life (to some extent), about why they engage in different activities and what they expect from it.
  3. Don't sleep and start now - the longer you wait the longer your way to success be.

    4. "Don't sleep" Photograph by Erika Lanskaronskyte

  4. Make contests – everyone loves to win and organizing contests is one of the best ways to make you followers engage with your contents. And don’t be upset if you don’t have resources to give actual presents yet. Sometimes people are way happier to get a shoutout or personalized video from you.
  5. Include a visual with every post – it will attract more attention and such post are more likely to be shared.
  6. Listen to your audience and respond to all the comments and feedbacks you get – this way you will have loyal and happy followers.
  7. Give people a reason to follow you – tell them how others benefit from the thing you do.
  8. Use hashtags – this way your profile will be easier to discover among all users.
  9. Be entertaining, use humor – people love having fun, although remember that your content always must be related with the main message you are giving to your followers.
"Opportunities don't happen. You create them."

These are all the tips we have for you today. We hope that they help you to build an engaging audience and become successful on social media.
